Want clickable ads?

If you want the whole ad box to link to a page (affiliate link, booking link, etc.) you can use:

""
<a href="https://your-ad-link.com" target="_blank">
  <img src="/ads/ad1.jpg" class="w-full h-full object-cover rounded-xl border border-gray-300" alt="Ad 1">
</a>

""
⭐ Want Google Ads later?

No problem — each ad file can easily be changed to:

""
<img src="/ads/ad1.jpg" class="w-full h-32 object-cover rounded-t-xl">

<script async src="https://pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js"></script>
<ins class="adsbygoogle"
     style="display:block"
     data-ad-client="ca-pub-XXXX"
     data-ad-slot="YYYY"
     data-ad-format="auto"></ins>
<script>
     (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
</script>

""


Your website already loads these files automatically on every page.
